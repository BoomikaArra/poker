Goal
Make the best 5-card hand (or bluff well enough) to win chips from other players.
Setup
Each player gets 2 private cards (called hole cards).
5 community cards are placed face up on the table (shared by everyone).
Players combine their 2 cards + community cards to make the best 5-card hand.
Gameplay Steps
Blinds:
Two players put in forced bets called small blind and big blind (to start the pot).
Pre-Flop:
Everyone gets 2 cards face down.
Betting starts (call, raise, or fold).
Flop:
3 community cards are dealt face up.
Another round of betting.
Turn:
A 4th community card is dealt.
Another round of betting.
River:
The 5th and final community card is dealt.
Final round of betting.
Showdown:
If 2 or more players remain, they reveal cards.
Best 5-card hand wins the pot
Player Actions
Action	Meaning
Fold	Quit the round; lose what you’ve bet.
Check	Pass your turn without betting (if no one bet yet).
Call	Match the current highest bet.
Raise	Increase the current bet.
